    • Log in

    In order to add projects or articles, you will need to be logged in. To do that, all you need is to click the "Log in" button on the navigation bar.

    This acction will lead you to the "Login" page. Now you can introduce your email and password here.

    After the fields are complited, press the "Login" blue button to finish the process. But if you want to cancel and go to the "Home" page, click the "Cancel" button.

    Registration

    To create a profile, click on "Sign in" button. Doing this, a registration page will be opened.

    Now you have to complite the field.

    If the fields are complited, press the "Sign in" blue button to finish the process. A new login page will be opened for you so you can log in.

    But if you want to cancel and go to the "Home" page, click the "Cancel" button. Furthemore, you can register with Facebook by clicking "FB" button.

    Profile

    The "Profile" page is available only to users already registered or logged in. In the top-right corner (navigation bar) the "Sign in" button has been repleaced after you have been logged with "Profile" button. Clicking this new button will lead you to profile page.

    You can add or edit your account information by presing the following icon.

    Each icon will open a dialog box with specific fields to complite or options to choose. For example:

    The setings icon button shows you two options regarding your account. Now you can edit your privacy settings or you can delete the account. Both of them will show dialog boxes with defferent options.

    The add icon buttons are used to add your own projects and articles to the profile. The new ones will be accessable for every user of the website. Those buttons will open new pages (fx. "Add your own article!" page).
